HUEVOS RANCHEROS

This authentic Huevos Rancheros recipe is guaranteed to quickly become a family favorite. A perfect breakfast dish, made with eggs, sunny side up, and topped with a sauce that is packed with roasted green chile, tomato, and onion. You're loved ones will certainly enjoy this dish anytime of the year!!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 4 corn tortillas, (can be substituted with flour)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup roasted green chile
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups refried beans
  • 4 eggs, cooked any style (recommended sunny side up)
  • Queso fresco

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat canola oil in a heavy skillet. Place tortillas carefully in the hot oil, one at a time, and fry for 20 to 40 seconds on each side (or until lightly crisp and golden brown)
  2. Saute onions in sauce pan with olive oil for 3 minutes
  3. Add tomatoes and chile, and saute for an additional 3 minutes. Add chicken broth. Stir everything together and bring to a simmer. Continue to cook until most of the liquid has been absorbed. About 7 to 10 minutes
  4. Assemble by placing a tostada (fried tortilla) on a serving plate; spoon on refried beans and top with a cooked egg, cooked salsa, and sprinkle with queso fresco
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!

NOTES

  • Yields: 4 servings
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Use store bought tostada shells for a shortcut
